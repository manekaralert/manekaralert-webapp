How It Works

One formula. Four signals.

No black boxes. No predictions. Just a transparent, rule-based model that classifies the market every trading day using a single calculation.

STEP 01

Daily Close Price

We track the Nifty 50 closing price every trading day from NSE historical data.

STEP 02

52-Week High

We track the rolling 52-week highest close — the reference point for measuring correction depth.

STEP 03

Correction %

One formula tells you exactly how far the market is from its recent peak.

((High − Close) ÷ High) × 100
STEP 04

Signal + Alert

The correction % maps to a signal level — and you get a WhatsApp or email alert instantly.
